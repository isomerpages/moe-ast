<h3>Objectives of the Educational Support Chapter</h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>The Educational Support (ES) Chapter is a professional network which aims
to promote a culture of professionalism and pride for teachers supporting
students who benefit from educational support. It serves as a focal point
for teachers to engage in collaborative learning and inquiry.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Through collaborations with schools, MOE divisions, and the National Institute
of Education (NIE), the Chapter aims to enhance teachers' Pedagogical Context
Knowledge (PCK) pertaining to motivation and engagement in learning.</p>
</li>
</ul>
